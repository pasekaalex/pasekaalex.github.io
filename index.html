I used the Kumo Library to construct a word cloud in the shape of a stairway for the lyrics of Stairway to Heaven by Led Zeppelin. command used to generate word cloud through terminal: kumo --input "lyrics.txt" --output "stairway.png" --color "(102,51,153),(178,140,217),(227,190,240)" --width 600 --height 498 --background "stairs.png" --collision pixel_perfect --font-type "Calibri"
